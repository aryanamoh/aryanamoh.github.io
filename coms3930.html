02.2024

Generative ♥️ Art

For this project, I wanted to create a dynamic piece of art using the Arduino platform and a TFT display. My goal was to create a cute piece of art with the generative technology of the ESP32.

This piece was displayed as part of a larger installation of ESP32s inside envelopes. To specialize my art to the space, I decided to create a ever-changing display of love poems. I found it fitting, given that the installation went up around Valentine's Day. The display gives a glimpse of a note that could be found inside a Valentine's Day card. I wanted to create a sense of romance and warmth through the combination of visual and textual elements.

One of the creative decisions I made was to use hearts as the main visual element in the artwork. Hearts, universally a symbols of love and affection, add a touch of playfulness to the overall composition. By randomly generating the size, location, and color of the hearts, I aimed to create movement and whimsy in the artwork.

In terms of the textual elements, I curated a collection of love poems that would be displayed on the screen. These poems were chosen to convey different aspects of love and to resonate with the viewers. By randomly selecting a poem from the collection and regularly changing it, I aimed to keep the piece engaging.

From a technical perspective, I encountered a few challenges while working on this project. One of the issues I faced was optimizing the performance of the display. Since the installation limited the size of batteries that could be used, I had to minimize energy usage throughout the installation. I did this by decreasing the backlight brightness and maximizing the number of black pixels in the display.

Overall, my creative decisions and technical considerations were motivated by my larger vision of creating a captivating and immersive generative art experience. By combining visual elements, textual content, and technical expertise, I aimed to create a piece of art that would evoke emotions, spark imagination, and leave a lasting impression on the viewers.
